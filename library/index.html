View our Video Library


Our video library is the place where you’ll find stories that can inspire you, stories you can relate to and stories that will hopefully encourage you to share your own experiences.  These videos include narratives of support, weight bias, “aha” moments, feelings of stress, loneliness, acceptance and more. These stories are here to show the full spectrum of what it’s like to live as a person affected by obesity and to remind us that we are not alone in this journey.
